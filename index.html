Rest Day
Light walk, stretch, hydrate well. Sleep 8h+. Your muscles grow on rest days.

Hydration

Drink 3–3.5L of water daily. Electrolytes matter — add a pinch of salt + lemon to one glass per day. Pre-workout: 500ml water, 30 min before.

Progressive Overload

Add reps or reduce rest time weekly. Once you can do 20+ clean reps, increase difficulty — slow negatives, harder variation, or weighted backpack.

Sleep

Aim for 8–9 hours. Growth hormone peaks in deep sleep. No screen 30 min before bed. Protein before sleep slows catabolism overnight.

Tracking Progress

Weigh yourself every Monday morning. Aim to gain 0.3–0.5 kg/month (lean bulk pace). Take monthly progress photos in the same lighting.

Warm-up

5–10 min always. Jumping jacks → arm circles → leg swings → 1 easy set of first exercise. Cold muscles tear. Don't skip this.

Chennai Climate

Humidity will spike your sweat rate. Work out early morning or post-sunset. Add coconut water or ORS drink on extra hot days for electrolytes.
